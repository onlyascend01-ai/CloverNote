Privacy Policy

Your data never leaves your machine.

Data Sovereignty

CloverNote is built on the principle of data sovereignty. We do not collect, store, or transmit your notes to any servers. All your thoughts are stored locally in an encrypted vault on your device.

Zero Telemetry

We do not track your usage, keystrokes, or application behavior. CloverNote is a pure local-first tool designed for absolute privacy.

Encryption

Notes are encrypted using AES-256 standards when a vault password is set. This encryption happens entirely on your local CPU.
