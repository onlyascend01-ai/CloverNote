Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data really local-only?

Yes. CloverNote does not have a central database. Your notes are stored in an encrypted vault directly on your device's hard drive.

How do I backup my notes?

You can manually copy your vault folder or use the "Export" feature to save specific notes as PDF or Markdown files.

What happens if I lose my password?

Because we don't store your data, we cannot reset your password. Please keep your Vault credentials in a safe place.
