Support

Common Questions.

How is my data encrypted?

Every vault uses AES-256-GCM encryption at rest. Your encryption key is derived locally using Argon2 and never leaves your CPU. Even if your hard drive is stolen, your notes remain unreadable without your master password.

What happens if I lose my password?

Because we have zero servers and zero backdoors, we cannot recover your password. We recommend storing your vault credentials in a physical password manager or hardware key.

Can I import my existing notes?

Yes. CloverNote supports mass import for Markdown and plain text files. Our native bridge ensures thousands of notes are indexed in milliseconds.
