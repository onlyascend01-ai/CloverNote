Our Mission

CloverNote was born out of a simple realization: our digital thoughts are increasingly becoming public property. In an era of mandatory cloud sync and data mining, we wanted to build a sanctuary.

We believe that privacy is a human right, not a feature. Our goal is to provide the most powerful, aesthetic, and secure writing environment on the planet.

Local First

We don't have servers for your notes. You own your vault entirely.

Aesthetic Driven

Writing is a creative act. Your workspace should inspire you.
