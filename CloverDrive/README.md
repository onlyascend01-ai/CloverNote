# CloverDrive

Part of the Clover Suite. A premium, sovereign alternative to cloud storage, focused on local-first data management and high-performance aesthetics.

## Vision
To provide a secure, native environment for managing files across all your devices without the privacy risks of traditional cloud providers.

## Features
- **Local-First Architecture**: Your files stay on your hardware.
- **Sovereign Encryption**: Military-grade security for your personal data.
- **Native Performance**: Built for speed and smooth interactions.
- **Cross-Platform**: Seamless experience on Desktop and Mobile.
