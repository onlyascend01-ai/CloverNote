Security Policy

Our Commitment

CloverNote is designed with a "Security by Default" architecture. Since your data never leaves your device, the traditional risks of cloud breaches and server-side leaks are effectively eliminated.

Encryption Standards

When you enable password protection, CloverNote uses AES-256-GCM encryption to secure your local vault. Encryption keys are generated using PBKDF2 with 100,000 iterations, ensuring brute-force resistance.

No Remote Access

We do not have a back-door to your data. There is no remote management console or recovery service. Your security is absolute, but this also means if you lose your password and don't have a backup, we cannot recover your data.

Reporting Vulnerabilities

If you discover a security vulnerability in CloverNote, we encourage you to report it to us immediately at security@clovernote.app. We appreciate responsible disclosure.
